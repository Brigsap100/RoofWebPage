Health & Benefits

Your Kodiak benefits at a glance — medical, dental, vision, retirement, and the programs that keep you and your family covered. The plan year runs January 1 through December 31.

Medical, Dental & Vision

Kodiak offers two medical plans through Anthem Blue Cross, plus dental (Delta Dental) and vision (VSP). Open enrollment runs November 1–30 each year. New hires enroll within 30 days of their start date.

PPO — Summit Plan

Broad national network with out-of-network coverage. Best if you want flexibility to see specialists without referrals.

  • No referrals required
  • Nationwide network
  • Higher premium, lower deductible

HMO — Base Plan

Lower cost with a primary-care-first model. Best if your providers are in-network and you want predictable copays.

  • Assigned primary care physician
  • Referrals for specialists
  • Lower premium, higher deductible

Dental & Vision

Delta Dental PPO covers 100% preventive, 80% basic, and 50% major services. VSP vision includes an annual exam and a $150 frame allowance.

  • $2,000 annual dental max
  • Orthodontia (child) covered 50%
  • $10 vision exam copay

Retirement & Savings

401(k) Match

Kodiak matches 100% of the first 3% you contribute, then 50% of the next 2% — up to a 4% company match.

  • Managed through Fidelity
  • Immediate eligibility, 3-year vesting on match
  • Roth and pre-tax options

HSA & FSA

Pair the PPO with a Health Savings Account, or use a Flexible Spending Account for eligible medical and dependent-care costs.

  • Kodiak seeds HSA with $500/year
  • 2026 FSA limit: $3,300
  • Dependent-care FSA up to $5,000

Life & Disability

Company-paid coverage plus voluntary buy-up options for you and your dependents.

  • Basic life: 1x salary (company paid)
  • Short-term disability: 60% of pay
  • Long-term disability available

Wellness & Support

Employee Assistance Program

Free, confidential support for you and your household — counseling, legal, financial, and work-life resources.

  • Up to 6 counseling sessions per issue
  • 24/7 hotline: 1-800-555-0142
  • No cost to employees

Wellness Program

Earn premium credits for completing an annual biometric screening and wellness activities.

  • Up to $300/year in premium credits
  • Gym reimbursement up to $40/month
  • Quarterly safety & wellness challenges

Field Crew Perks

Benefits built for the trade — because most of the team works on the roof, not at a desk.

  • Annual boot & PPE allowance
  • Telehealth included on both plans
  • Bilingual benefits support
