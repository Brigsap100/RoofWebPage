Time Off (PTO)

How paid time off accrues, the 2026 holiday schedule, and how to request the days you need. Submit requests at least two weeks ahead when you can.

Accrual Policy

Full-time employees accrue paid time off each pay period based on years of service. PTO covers vacation and personal days; sick leave accrues separately per state law. Up to 40 hours of unused PTO carries into the next year.

2026 Company Holidays

Kodiak observes 10 paid holidays. When a holiday falls on a weekend, it is observed on the nearest weekday. Field crews on active emergency work may be scheduled with holiday pay.

  • New Year's Day — Thu, Jan 1
  • Martin Luther King Jr. Day — Mon, Jan 19
  • Presidents' Day — Mon, Feb 16
  • Memorial Day — Mon, May 25
  • Juneteenth — Fri, Jun 19
  • Independence Day — Fri, Jul 3 (observed)
  • Labor Day — Mon, Sep 7
  • Thanksgiving Day — Thu, Nov 26
  • Day After Thanksgiving — Fri, Nov 27
  • Christmas Day — Fri, Dec 25

How To Request Time Off

The Process

  1. Submit your request below (or in ADP) at least 2 weeks ahead
  2. Your foreman or manager reviews for crew coverage
  3. You'll get an approval notice by email
  4. Approved time posts to your ADP balance

Good To Know

  • Sick leave doesn't need advance notice — notify your foreman ASAP
  • Blackout periods may apply during peak season
  • Bereavement: up to 3 paid days for immediate family
  • Check your balance in ADP → Myself → Time Off
