Pay Schedule

Kodiak pays bi-weekly (every other Friday), 26 pay periods a year. Each check covers the two-week period ending the Sunday before payday. Timecards are due Monday at 10:00 AM. If a payday lands on a holiday, funds deposit the prior business day.

How To Access Your Documents

Pay Stubs

Stubs post to the ADP Workforce Now employee portal by the Wednesday before each payday.

  1. Sign in at workforcenow.adp.com
  2. Go to Myself → Pay → Pay Statements
  3. View or download any stub as PDF

W-2s & Tax Forms

Annual W-2s are available online by January 31 and mailed to your address on file.

  1. Myself → Pay → Tax Statements
  2. Opt in to paperless for earliest access
  3. Prior years stay available for 3 years

First-Time Login

Haven't set up ADP yet? You'll need the company registration code.

  1. Click "Create Account" on the ADP login
  2. Registration code: Kodiak-2026
  3. Verify with your SSN and date of hire

Direct Deposit

Direct deposit is standard for all employees. You can split pay across up to three accounts. Changes take effect the pay period after they're verified (allow one cycle for pre-note).

Set Up or Change

  1. ADP → Myself → Pay → Payment Options
  2. Add your bank routing and account numbers
  3. Choose amount, percentage, or remainder per account
  4. Save — a voided-check upload speeds verification

Good To Know

  • New accounts pre-note for one cycle
  • During pre-note you'll get a paper check
  • Update ASAP after switching banks
  • Pay cards available on request

Timecard Reminders

  • Field crews clock in/out in the ADP mobile app
  • Approve your timecard by Monday 10:00 AM
  • Foremen approve crew hours by Monday noon
  • Missed punches: notify your foreman same day
